### Content Gap Summary

- **No trading academy has a single short overview covering Market Profile + Volume Profile + Order Flow as one topic family.** Competitor pages pick one tool and go deep, or treat them as rival tools to compare. A concise "family roadmap" (what each tool is + why it matters) is unclaimed.
- Most competitor content skips trader psychology behind volume clusters — why price stalls at HVNs and sprints through LVNs in terms of participant behaviour.
- Risk management + practical limitations of profile-based tools are consistently underweighted. A realistic framing ("these tools show where trading happened, not where it will happen next") is a differentiator.
- Forex applicability is briefly mentioned but rarely explored — most content assumes futures/equities where true volume is available.
- The Lekce format (teaser + roadmap to 5 chapters) is a natural fit for this gap: it positions each chapter as one angle on the same auction-theory foundation.
